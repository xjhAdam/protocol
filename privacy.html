Privacy Policy for App Lock Guard

Release Date: March 04, 2026
Effective Date: March 04, 2026

App Lock Guard ("we", "our", or "the application") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

App Lock Guard is a local utility application. Most data is processed and stored locally on your device.

  • Locked application list (stored locally only)
  • Encrypted password or pattern data (stored locally only)
  • Intruder selfie photos if enabled (stored locally only)

We do not collect or store your personal files, contacts, or messages.

2. Permissions Used

Usage Access Permission

This permission is used to detect which application is currently running in order to apply app lock protection. The data is processed locally and is not uploaded to our servers.

Camera Permission

If the Intruder Selfie feature is enabled, the application may use the camera to take a photo when an incorrect password is entered. The photo is stored locally on your device only.

Storage Permission

Used to save configuration data and intruder photos locally on the device.

3. Third-Party Services

1. Bugly Crash Reporting (Tencent)

We use Bugly to collect crash logs in order to improve application stability.

  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • Application version
  • Crash log information

2. Google AdMob

The application uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

AdMob may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information
  • IP address
  • App interaction data
  • Network information

Advertising data is processed by Google according to their privacy policy.

You can learn more at:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information.

Information may only be shared with third-party service providers listed in this policy for the purpose of improving the application or displaying advertisements.

5. Data Storage

  • Application lock data is stored locally on your device
  • Intruder photos remain on the device
  • Crash logs may be uploaded to Bugly servers
  • Advertising data may be processed by Google AdMob

6. Children's Privacy

This application is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain privacy rights under laws such as GDPR or CCPA. These may include:

  • The right to access your data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to restrict processing

Since most data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it by uninstalling the application or clearing the app data.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the application or on the official page.
